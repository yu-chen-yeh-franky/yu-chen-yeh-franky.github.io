Frequently Asked Questions

SkipFrog is a Chrome extension that lets you mark custom time segments in any YouTube video and automatically skip or loop them during playback. It's perfect for jumping past unwanted tracks in long compilation or DJ mix videos, skipping intros and sponsored content, or repeating your favorite sections on demand.
Yes. SkipFrog for YouTube is completely free to install and use from the Chrome Web Store.
Yes. You can define multiple custom segments per video and mix skip and loop behaviors so that long mixes, compilations, or lectures play back exactly the way you want.
  • Skipping tracks you don't like in hour-long DJ mixes or music compilations
  • Jumping past intros, outros, and sponsored segments
  • Looping a favorite chorus, dance move, or tutorial step on repeat
  • Cleaning up rewatch experiences for long-form content
On the first install, Chrome may display a security warning because this is a newly published extension that hasn't yet built up an established reputation. Simply click "Continue to install" and the extension will work normally.
SkipFrog is intentionally lightweight and runs only on YouTube pages, so the impact on browser performance and video playback is minimal.
No. I don't collect or use your data — SkipFrog handles your custom segments locally in your browser, without tracking your activity.
