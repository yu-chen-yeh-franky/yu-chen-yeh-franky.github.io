Frequently Asked Questions

You can report bugs by sending an email to franky.2.0808@gmail.com or by filling out our support form. Please include your device model, OS version, app version, and a description of the issue.
We aim to respond to all support requests within 1–3 business days. Complex issues may take a bit longer, but we'll keep you updated.
We'd love to hear your ideas! Send your feature request via email or the support form. While we can't promise every request will be implemented, all feedback is carefully reviewed.
Privacy policy details are available on each app's individual page. In general, our apps do not collect personal data. Your privacy is our priority.
Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com. For Google Play purchases, visit the Google Play Help Center.
